Research Interest

My research interest lies in embodied AI, particularly robotics, computer vision, and natural language processing with the primary goal of achieving general intelligent robotic action currrently from two main perspectives:

  • Use language and vision (possibly audio) foundation models to help robots fully understand the world.
  • Help robots interact with the environment with intelligent agents to learn more general policies.

Ultimately, my goal is to enable robots to understand human language and visual environment, then assist humans in unstructured environments, particularly at life and work scenarios. To achieve this, many of my works currently consider tasks in robotic reasoning and planning, which I think are the foundations for robotic manipulation.
